Applied microeconomist

Yuhang Pan

I study environmental regulation, health policy, and development using applied microeconomic methods.

I am a tenure-track assistant professor at the Institute for Global Health and Development at Peking University. My research examines how public policies shape environmental quality, health, and incentives in health care systems.
