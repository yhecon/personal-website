Prospective Students and RAs

I keep recruiting PhD students and research assistants. Ideal candidates should be trained in economics with strong analytical skills. Students proficient in Stata, R, ArcGIS, Python, and machine learning will be given priority.

Please email me your CV and a two-page research proposal, working paper, or published work if you are interested: yhpan@pku.edu.cn. I will not respond to emails that only attach a CV.
